About katherineblower

Joined June 2016 • 504 designs


I am an illustrator and designer based in the West Midlands with a First Class degree with Hons in Visual Communication with a specialism in illustration. I am heavily influenced by nature and Scandinavian design and use a variety of techniques within my work called digital collage using all manner of materials from photographed textures, patterns and even textiles.
